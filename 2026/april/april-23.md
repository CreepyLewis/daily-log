## 🐙 GitHub — Stars & Achievements

### TIL: Making a repo private permanently deletes all stars!
Even if you make it public again — the stars are gone forever and cannot be recovered.

**Lesson:** Never make a public repo with stars private. Ever! 🔒

---

### TIL: GitHub achievements are permanent!
Once you earn an achievement it never goes away — even if:
- Your streak breaks
- You lose stars
- You delete repos

**Lesson:** Grind achievements without fear — they stick forever! 🏆

---

### TIL: Heart On Your Sleeve & Open Sourcerer are not released yet
These two achievements exist in GitHub's code but haven't been released to users yet.

**Lesson:** Don't waste time trying to earn unreleased achievements! ⏳

---

### TIL: Co-authored commits credit both people automatically
Using `Co-authored-by: username <username@users.noreply.github.com>` in a commit description automatically credits your friend — they don't need to do anything!

**Lesson:** Great way to earn Pair Extraordinaire with friends! 🤝
